Ms. KAMLA PAL Principal, Seth Anandram Jaipuria School Sadhu Campus , Barabanki

As you explore our offerings, you’ll discover answers to your initial inquiries about our school, academic programs, and student life. Serving as the Principal, I am honored to be part of an educational institution where every stakeholder is a learner, and each day presents an opportunity for discovery. We view ourselves as a community of learners, where teachers, parents, and staff are actively engaged in the learning process.

Maintaining high standards, I am committed to understanding and enhancing the educational process. We employ a dual strategy approach—Love and Logic—to create a positive learning environment. Love fosters trusting relationships, ensuring students feel respected and valued, while Logic instills personal responsibility, self-control, decision-making skills, self-confidence, and character-building with high moral values.

Your child’s learning journey is a collaborative effort between home and school. We believe that a strong partnership enhances your child’s overall experience. Learning is inherently social, and I eagerly anticipate working with you to create a school where parents are always welcome, students engage in meaningful learning, and staff members are valued for their contributions.

Together, we aim to uphold a standard of excellence, promote a positive learning atmosphere, and foster an environment where everyone can thrive. I look forward to the collective efforts of our entire community as we embark on this educational journey together.
